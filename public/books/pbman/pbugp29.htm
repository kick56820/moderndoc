Chapter 4 PowerDesigner Integration

About this chapter

PowerBuilder provides a plug-in interface that you can use to link a PowerBuilder target to a PowerDesigner object-oriented model. This chapter describes how to enable the plug-in and use it to generate or reverse-engineer PowerBuilder targets and objects.
